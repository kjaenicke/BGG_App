iBGG

iBGG is an unofficial BGG reference app. It was designed to be used as a quick and easy reference for anyone to find information about their favorite board games. With the ability to bookmark games, you always have your favorite games at your fingertips.

View the Project on GitHub

Features:

  • Search functionality to find your favorite games.
  • Bookmarks to keep track of your games.
  • Game details including comments, forums, and images.
  • BGG Top 100 and Hot Games lists.
  • Designed for iOS.
